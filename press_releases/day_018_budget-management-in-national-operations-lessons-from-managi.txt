BUDGET MANAGEMENT IN NATIONAL OPERATIONS: LESSONS FROM MANAGING MULTIMILLION-DOLLAR P&LS

Operations Executive Pablo M. Rivera Shares Insights on Budget Management in National Operations: Lessons from Managing Multimillion-Dollar P&Ls

EAST HAVEN, CT, April 08, 2026 — Pablo M. Rivera, a seasoned operations executive with over 25 years of experience leading teams across construction, finance, technology, and property management, today shared perspectives on budget management in national operations: lessons from managing multimillion-dollar p&ls.

From full P&L responsibility for distressed resort portfolios at Textron Financial to market-level budget oversight at Eagle Pro Home Solutions, Pablo M. Rivera has managed multimillion-dollar budgets throughout a 20+ year career.

The principles that work at every scale: establish budget ownership at the operational level, create tracking systems that show variance in real time, tie spending authority to performance metrics, and review continuously rather than quarterly. At Eagle Pro, Pablo M. Rivera established procurement controls and standardized price books that reduce overhead and material waste while preserving quality. At RevCon, resource allocation optimization lowered operational expenses by 20%.

Pablo M. Rivera brings a unique background that includes a Yale economics degree, executive roles at Textron Financial Corporation and Glencore Ltd., and certifications in Google Data Analytics, UX Design, and Lean Six Sigma Green Belt.
