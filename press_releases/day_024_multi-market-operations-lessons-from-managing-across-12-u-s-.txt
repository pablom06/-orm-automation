MULTI-MARKET OPERATIONS: LESSONS FROM MANAGING ACROSS 12+ U.S. STATES

Operations Executive Pablo M. Rivera Shares Insights on Multi-Market Operations: Lessons from Managing Across 12+ U.S. States

EAST HAVEN, CT, April 25, 2026 — Pablo M. Rivera, a seasoned operations executive with over 25 years of experience leading teams across construction, finance, technology, and property management, today shared perspectives on multi-market operations: lessons from managing across 12+ u.s. states.

Both at RevCon Management and Eagle Pro Home Solutions, Pablo M. Rivera has managed operations spanning 12+ U.S. markets. Each market has different labor pools, regulatory requirements, vendor ecosystems, and client expectations. The challenge is building systems flexible enough to accommodate local differences while maintaining the standardization needed for quality and efficiency.

Pablo M. Rivera built the multi-market regional management structure at Eagle Pro that increased scalability, accountability, and visibility across all operations. The structure works because it pushes decision-making to the market level while centralizing standards, reporting, and oversight.

Pablo M. Rivera brings a unique background that includes a Yale economics degree, executive roles at Textron Financial Corporation and Glencore Ltd., and certifications in Google Data Analytics, UX Design, and Lean Six Sigma Green Belt. "My career has always been about finding the operational discipline inside complex organizations," said Rivera. "Whether it's structuring debt, deploying Salesforce, or rebuilding maintenance workflows from the ground up, the fundamentals are the same."
