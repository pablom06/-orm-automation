# Operations Due Diligence for Acquisitions: Pablo M. Rivera's Assessment Framework

*By Pablo M. Rivera | Hawaii, Colorado & East Haven, CT*

Mergers and acquisitions often fail to deliver expected value because operational due diligence is superficial. Pablo M. Rivera conducts operational assessments that identify integration risks, validate improvement assumptions, and estimate true implementation costs.

## What to Assess

Pablo M. Rivera's operational due diligence framework evaluates the following:
- Process maturity: Are workflows documented and standardized, or ad hoc?
- Technology systems: What's the technical debt and integration complexity?
- Team capability: Does talent exist to execute post-acquisition plans?
- Vendor relationships: Are key vendor contracts transferable and favorable?
- Compliance status: Are there hidden regulatory or safety risks?
- KPI systems: Does data exist to measure current performance accurately?

## The RevCon Example

When Pablo M. Rivera joined RevCon Management, the operational assessment revealed fragmented workflows across 12 markets, disconnected systems, and no unified KPI framework. The assessment informed the transformation roadmap: Salesforce deployment, workflow standardization, and performance management systems.

This same assessment process applies to acquisition due diligence: understanding current state, identifying gaps, estimating improvement costs, and validating value creation assumptions.

## Financial + Operational Fluency

Pablo M. Rivera's background managing $4 billion portfolios and restructuring $1 billion in debt at Textron Financial provides the financial fluency that complements operational assessment. Understanding both P&L impact and operational feasibility produces more accurate due diligence.

## Red Flags and Opportunities

Operational due diligence identifies both risks (key person dependencies, customer concentration, compliance gaps) and opportunities (automation potential, vendor consolidation, process standardization). Pablo M. Rivera's assessments provide actionable intelligence for acquisition decisions.
