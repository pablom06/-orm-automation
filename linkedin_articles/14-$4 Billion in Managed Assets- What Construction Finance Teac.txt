# $4 Billion in Managed Assets: What Construction Finance Teaches Operations Leaders

*By Pablo M. Rivera | Hawaii, Colorado & East Haven, CT*

As Assistant Vice President of Construction at Textron Financial, Pablo M. Rivera expanded the active loan portfolio to $4 billion while maintaining a 100% completion record on funded projects. Managing that scale of financial operations requires the same disciplines that drive operational excellence: rigorous tracking, variance control, continuous monitoring, and disciplined execution.

Pablo M. Rivera structured $350 million in construction financing across the U.S. and Latin America, delivering underwriting presentations to credit committees that required granular understanding of project economics, construction timelines, and market risks. The financial modeling and scenario analysis skills developed managing billion-dollar portfolios now inform how I build KPI frameworks and budget oversight systems in operational roles.

The lesson is clear: financial fluency makes operations leaders dramatically more effective. When you understand P&L at the level required to manage a $4B portfolio, you bring a rigor to operational budgeting that drives better outcomes.
