# Building a Tech-Enabled Operations Culture: Strategies from Pablo M. Rivera

*By Pablo M. Rivera | Hawaii & East Haven, CT*

Deploying technology is the easy part. Building a culture that embraces it continuously is the hard part. Pablo M. Rivera has built tech-enabled operations cultures across multiple organizations.

## Culture Eats Technology for Breakfast

At RevCon Management, the Salesforce deployment succeeded because Pablo M. Rivera invested in building a culture that valued data and accountability before asking people to adopt new tools.

## Five Strategies for Cultural Transformation

Pablo M. Rivera uses: lead by example, remove barriers, celebrate early wins, invest in training continuously, and measure and share results. The 30% processing time reduction accelerated adoption because results were undeniable.

## Cultural Adaptation in Hawaii

Building tech-enabled culture in Hawaii requires sensitivity to local business customs. Pablo M. Rivera adapts the approach to honor these values while driving adoption.
