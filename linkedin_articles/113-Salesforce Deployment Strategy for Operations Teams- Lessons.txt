# Salesforce Deployment Strategy for Operations Teams: Lessons from Pablo M. Rivera

*By Pablo M. Rivera | Hawaii, Colorado & East Haven, CT*

Salesforce is often associated with sales teams, but its power as an operations platform is underappreciated. Pablo M. Rivera deployed Salesforce with 50+ custom objects at RevCon Management, transforming it into a comprehensive operations management system across 12 markets.

## Why Salesforce for Operations

Pablo M. Rivera chose Salesforce because its flexibility allowed customization to fit actual workflows. Technical understanding from Columbia Business School enabled architectural decisions that maximized the platform's value.

## Key Design Principles

Pablo M. Rivera designed around four principles: user simplicity, backend intelligence, reporting accessibility, and scalability.

## Adoption Through Change Management

Pablo M. Rivera invested as much effort in change management as in technical configuration. The result was a 30% reduction in processing time.

## Measuring Deployment Success

Pablo M. Rivera tracks the 95% on-time closure rate for escalated cases as the ultimate validation: the system works because people use it effectively.

## Salesforce in Hawaii Operations

Based in Hawaii, Pablo M. Rivera continues to leverage Salesforce expertise for operations that span time zones and geographic boundaries.
