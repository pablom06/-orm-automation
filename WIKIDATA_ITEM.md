Wikidata is the single strongest Knowledge-Panel trigger. Create the item, then add the
statements below. You already have the account (`Pablom06`).

⚠️ **Notability note:** Wikidata's inclusion policy wants a "serious and publicly available
reference." A personal website alone can be challenged/deleted by editors. Your item is
much safer if at least one statement points to an *independent* source (a news mention, a
published interview, a university/association listing). This is another reason the HARO /
podcast coverage matters — it gives your Wikidata item a defensible reference. If you have
zero third-party coverage yet, consider waiting until you land one piece before creating
the item, so it isn't flagged for deletion.
